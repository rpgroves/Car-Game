-driving game 2d top down
-you are a taxi driver
-you free roam to find riders
-you have a compass telling you where to go
-crashing deducts money you make
-you can fill your car with gas and upgrade it
-pedestrians and other cars move around the area

	and going at green lights
	-pedestrians cross in front of cars that have red lights
